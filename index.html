About PSR Constructions

PSR Group was established in 2000 and is one of the leading real estate companies in Bangalore. We have developed over 4 million sqft of residential, commercial and residential plots in the glorious 20+ years across prime locations in Bangalore.

The PSR Group has to its credit a number of completed projects. To name a few there are PSR Arcade, PSR Pavilion, PSR Flora, PSR Garden, PSR Aster and many more in the list of 19 projects developed to date. Innovative construction and futuristic infrastructure is our forte.
